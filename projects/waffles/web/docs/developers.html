How to become a Waffles developer

Basically, just make a useful contribution to the project, and then you are a Waffles developer. That's all there is to it. We'll be happy to give you commit access to our version-control repository, and add you as a developer to our SourceForge page, etc. If you need some help getting to that point, send me email. (My email address is on the main page.)
